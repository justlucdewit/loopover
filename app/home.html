Welcome to Loopover!

Loopover is a 2D Rubik's Cube-like puzzle originally developed
by Cary Huang (from the Youtube channel CaryKH).

There is a community of about 250 people on Discord or Reddit
(join us!). You can also play the game, read about the game, and check out the leaderboards on this site.
